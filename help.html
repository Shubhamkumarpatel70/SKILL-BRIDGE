FAQ and Help

What is Skills Bridge?

Skills Bridge is an online platform that connects students with diverse internship opportunities across various domains.

How do I apply for an internship on Skills Bridge?

To apply for an internship, you need to register first on our platform, browse available internships, and submit your application through the specified process.

What types of internships are available on Skills Bridge?

Skills Bridge offers internships in various fields, including technology, business, marketing, and design. The available internships may vary. Click here for more details...

How are interns selected for opportunities on Skills Bridge?

Interns are selected based on their qualifications, experience, and how well they match the requirements outlined by the internship posting.

Can I apply for multiple internships simultaneously?

Yes, you can apply for multiple internships in different domains at the same time.

How long do internships on Skills Bridge typically last?

The duration of internships varies. Some are short-term, while others may extend to several months. The duration will be specified in the internship posting.

Can I intern with Skills Bridge if I am an international student?

Yes, Skills Bridge welcomes international students to apply for internships. However, certain internships may have specific eligibility criteria.

How can I provide feedback or report issues on the Skills Bridge platform?

You can provide feedback or report issues by contacting our support team through the contact section.

Can I apply for internships if I am still in my first year of college?

Yes, Skills Bridge welcomes applications from students in various academic years. Some internships may have specific eligibility criteria, so make sure to check the requirements for each opportunity.

How do I register on Skills Bridge?

To register on Skills Bridge, simply click on the registration button and fill out the basic details.

Does Skills Bridge provide any certificates?

Yes, Skills Bridge provides both offer letters and completion certificates.

How do I access my internship tasks on Skills Bridge?

You can access your internship tasks by logging into your dashboard and navigating to the 'Tasks' section. From there, you can view and download your assigned tasks.

Can I extend my internship duration on Skills Bridge?

Extensions are subject to approval by the organization offering the internship. You can request an extension by contacting the organization directly through your dashboard.

How do I contact support for technical issues on Skills Bridge?

If you encounter technical issues, you can reach out to our support team through the contact page or email us directly.

What should I do if I miss a task deadline on Skills Bridge?

If you miss a task deadline, you should immediately inform your internship coordinator and request a possible extension. However, timely submission is highly encouraged.
